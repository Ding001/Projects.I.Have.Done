Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)



Q: Why have a website about happiness?



A. Happiness plays a major part in everyone's lives. We tend to forget how important it is and how it affects our thoughts and behaviours. If we are happy, we feel more energetic, more creative, more optimistic and this is reflected in our actions. The more good we do, the more positivism and hope we ing to the world and to ourselves.


We hoped that by having a website only about happiness would reinforce the importance it has on our lives.



Q: How did the “The Happiness Equation” come up?



A. This website was created by 4 undergraduate students at the University of Sydney. They, after being amazed by a subject they took together, thought of applying the concepts learnt to create a website which could help people understand happiness and hopefully make them happier.



Q: What are the main objectives of “The Happiness Equation”?



  • Make happiness an approachable topic people can talk about and discuss.
  • Build a community with positive thinking that can support each other.
  • Track people’s happiness level over time.
  • Achieve a happier world.



Q: How can I be benefited?



A. Each user by completing different tasks and actions will be awarded with medals to keep record of the good actions they have done so far. There is also a challenge users can participate once called “My Happy Moments”. Users upload a picture each day about something that made them happy for 100 days consecutively and they can have all their pictures printed and delivered for free.



Q: Are there are any requirements for joining?



A. Anyone can create an account and join our community. There’s no age limit or requirements. If you are interested in discovering your own happiness level, how it is affected and achieve a happier life, join us NOW. It’s free!



Q: Will the website make me happier?



A. The purpose of the website is to understand oneself and enrich the mind with knowledge about happiness. It is a journey with no final destination. Through the journey, one can do different quizzes that tackle different areas related to happiness, have a list of good actions to achieve, share moments of happiness with others, read interesting articles based on research and others experiences in life, among others. We believe that each one of these components will help one understand themselves better are and aim to increase their happiness level.



Q: What is the best way to measure happiness?



A. There are more than only one variable that affects happiness and each one of us define happiness in a different way.
